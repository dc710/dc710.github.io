The Role of AI in Threat Detection

AI systems are specifically designed to analyse patterns and anomalies that could indicate a potential threat, ranging from basic malware to complex cyber-attacks. With the ability to process enormous amounts of data at high speeds, AI can identify potential threats much faster and more accurately than traditional methods. In this section, we will discuss the mechanisms of AI-based threat detection, which include machine learning algorithms, anomaly detection, and predictive analytics.

AI-Driven Response Strategies

When a cyber threat is detected, the time it takes to respond is crucial. AI can improve response times by automating actions like isolating affected systems, deploying patches, and learning from past incidents to prevent future breaches. This section will examine different AI-driven response strategies and how they help reduce cyber risks.
